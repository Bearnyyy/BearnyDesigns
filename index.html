Bearny : Model and Texture Artist

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Bearny - a freelance Minecraft texture and 3D model artist based in Latvia.

Capes

I started my creative journey in May of 2021 by making free capes for a wrapping growing community.

Currently, I am working with two companies where my works can be found: Cloaks+ and Mantle.

Cosmetics

Soon after tested out some new waters with Blockbench modeling and cosmetic creating. Initially, the designs were meant for personal resource packs, but afterward I started making holiday and pop-culture-themed collections.
